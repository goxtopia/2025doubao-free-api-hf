Child Study Focus Assistant

Technique: Gaze Tracking / Pose Estimation

1. User Pain Point

Parents worry if kids are doing homework or playing on phones during study time.

2. Abstract Need

Productivity monitoring for dependents.

3. Initial Solution

Camera tracks child's gaze and posture at the desk. Alerts parents if 'phone use' > 10 mins.

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Draconian and invasive. Destroys trust between parent and child.

5. Refined Strategy

Reframe as a 'Focus Coach' for the child. Gamify it. 'You focused for 40 mins! Take a break.' Report trends to parents, not instant snitching.

6. Final Product Feature

Homework Gamification Engine: Tracks 'Deep Work' time for kids, rewarding them with screen time credits for actual study hours.
