Automated Pet Training Assistant

Technique: Action Recognition / Audio Synthesis

1. User Pain Point

Dogs jump on the couch or scratch doors when owners aren't looking.

2. Abstract Need

Negative reinforcement/correction in absence of authority figure.

3. Initial Solution

Camera detects 'Dog on Couch'. Speaker immediately plays a pre-recorded 'No!' or ultrasonic sound.

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Latency makes this ineffective (dog must connect action to sound). False positives scare the dog confusingly.

5. Refined Strategy

Low-latency edge processing is key. Use positive reinforcement too (dispense treat if dog goes to bed). Log events for owner review.

6. Final Product Feature

Pavlov’s Sentry: A training tool that provides consistent, immediate feedback to pets for specific boundary zones, maintaining training consistency 24/7.
