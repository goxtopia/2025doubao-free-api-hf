Garden Health Sentry

Technique: Fine-grained Image Classification / Segmentation

1. User Pain Point

Plants die while owners are on vacation; pests destroy gardens unnoticed.

2. Abstract Need

Agricultural monitoring scaled down to domestic use.

3. Initial Solution

Outdoor cams scan plants for drooping leaves (water stress) or discoloration (disease/pests).

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Outdoor lighting varies wildly. Wind moves plants. Resolution at distance is poor.

5. Refined Strategy

Use 'fixed tour' snapshots (camera zooms to specific presets daily). Compare relative change day-over-day rather than absolute classification.

6. Final Product Feature

Gardener Assistant: Daily report flagging 'Zone A needs water' or 'Suspicious yellow spots on Rose bush', prompting manual check.
