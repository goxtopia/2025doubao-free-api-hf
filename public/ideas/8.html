Noise Pollution & Sleep Hygiene Logger

Technique: Audio Event Classification + Time Series Analysis

1. User Pain Point

Poor sleep quality, unsure if it's internal (snoring) or external (cars, neighbors).

2. Abstract Need

Correlation of environmental factors with biological rest.

3. Initial Solution

Microphone logs decibel spikes and classifies source (Siren, Dog, Snore, Trash Truck).

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Audio recording inside the bedroom is a massive privacy taboo.

5. Refined Strategy

Process locally. Do not save raw audio. Save only metadata events (e.g., '03:00 AM - Loud Truck - 70dB'). Correlate with existing sleep tracker data.

6. Final Product Feature

Sleep Disruptor Audit: A morning dashboard showing what noises occurred during your deep sleep phases to help soundproof the room.
