Fire Risk Inspector

Technique: Visual Relationship Detection (Scene Graph)

1. User Pain Point

Fires often start from negligence (towel on space heater, candle near curtain) before smoke detectors trigger.

2. Abstract Need

Proactive hazard identification based on spatial relationships.

3. Initial Solution

Scan rooms for 'Dangerous Pairs': {Heater, Cloth}, {Candle, Curtain}, {Curling Iron, Carpet}.

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Context matters (heater might be off). 3D depth needed to confirm proximity.

5. Refined Strategy

Integrate with smart plug data (is heater on?). Use depth estimation. Alert user: 'Hazard detected: Cloth object dangerously close to active heater.'

6. Final Product Feature

Pre-Ignition Hazard Alert: Visual auditing of physical spaces for fire safety compliance, alerting users to move objects before they catch fire.
