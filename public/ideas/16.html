Rodent Incursion Mapper

Technique: Motion Accumulation / Small Object Detection

1. User Pain Point

You see mouse droppings but don't know where they are coming in.

2. Abstract Need

Pest control intelligence.

3. Initial Solution

Night vision camera tracks fast, small movements. Mapping all motion vectors over a week.

4. Self-Correction / Refutation

Mice are fast and blurry. Individual clips are hard to spot.

5. Refined Strategy

Don't just alert. Overlay all movement paths onto a single static image ('Heatmap'). Highlight entry/exit points (holes in baseboards).

6. Final Product Feature

Vermin Vector Map: A visualization tool showing exactly which crack in the wall the mice are using, so you can seal it effectively.
